My journey to becoming a web developer has been quite a ride. I feel that I should start at the point when my interest of computers peaked. I considered majoring in something related to computers after graduating high school, but chose to major in Mathematics, which is my favorite subject. I changed my major years later to Computer Science because I wanted to learn coding and pursue a career in this field.
I am currently in a Software/Web Development course through Correlation One, sponsored by Amazon. I joined this course due to my interest in Computer Science. We started out with learning HTML, which was fun. We have just finished learned about CSS, which was exciting as it makes styling our pages SO MUCH better and easier!
